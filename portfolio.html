About Me

I am an experienced BIM professional with 12 years of expertise delivering high-profile infrastructure and building projects across the Middle East, Europe, and Asia.

My specialization covers the end-to-end BIM lifecycle, including ISO 19650 implementation, BIM Execution Planning (BEP), and complex inter-disciplinary coordination.

I have a proven track record across diverse sectors such as metro systems, data centers, healthcare, and commercial buildings. I am adept at driving digital efficiency through custom workflows, clash detection, and model optimization to enhance project efficiency, reduce costs, and mitigate risks.
